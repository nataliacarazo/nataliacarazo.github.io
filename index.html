Natalia Carazo

Wedding Planner and Coordinator

Hi! My name is Natalia Carazo — I’m a Wedding Planner and Coordinator based in Southern California. With years of experience in event planning and coordination, I’ve had the privilege of bringing hundreds of special occasions to life, from intimate ceremonies to large-scale weddings. My work combines creative design, logistics management, and client collaboration to create meaningful celebrations that reflect each couple’s unique story.

As part of the administration team and lead planner of an event coordination business, I handle every aspect of the process — from venue research and vendor communication to design concepts and detailed timelines. My goal is to make each event as seamless as possible so my clients can focus on enjoying their day. I believe that clear communication, preparation, and kindness are the foundation of a successful event.

Outside of wedding planning, I enjoy connecting with other professionals in the event industry, developing creative projects, and supporting community events. Whether it’s coordinating a corporate celebration or helping a family plan a milestone party, I’m passionate about turning visions into memorable experiences. My mission is to create moments that last a lifetime, one event at a time.

Over the years, I’ve collaborated with talented vendors, photographers, florists, and venues to design cohesive event experiences that bring each couple’s vision to life. My approach blends creativity with organization — from detailed day-of timelines and décor coordination to seamless communication with clients and vendors. I’m proud of creating events that not only look beautiful but also feel effortless for everyone involved.

I’m continually expanding my expertise through event management training, design workshops, and real-world experience. Whether I’m planning a modern celebration in Los Angeles or an intimate ceremony in Orange County, I strive to make every event memorable and stress-free. My ultimate goal is to continue growing as a creative professional while helping couples celebrate love in the most authentic and joyful way possible.
